1. When the booking is completed, the system shall create an event:
	a. on customer’s Google Calendar at the booked day and hour, specifying the saloon’s address.
	b. on business’s Google Calendar at the customer’s booked day and hour, specifying the customer’s name.

2. When the booking is completed, the system shall send a confirmation e-mail, where address and hour are specified, to the customer.

3. During the registration process, the system shall ask to the user if it is a costumer or a business.
